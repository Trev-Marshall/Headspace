What is a 529 plan?

A 529 Savings Plan is a state-sponsored investment account that has tax advantages when used to save for education.


Tax-free investing

  • Contributions to a 529 plan grow tax-free and can be withdrawn tax-free to pay for education.

Flexible and Versatile

  • 529s can be used for higher education, K-12, apprenticeships, and student loans. You can even transfer within the family.

Better for financial aid

  • Unlike UGMA/UTMA, a 529 plan will have a minimal impact on your child’s financial aid eligibility.
